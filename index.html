Application-specific computer systemmsd

What is an embedded system?（什么是嵌入式系统）

  1. Application-specific computer system（专用的计算机系统

  2. Built into a larger system(嵌入到更大的系统之中)

  3. Often with real-time computing constraints(通常具有实时计算约束)

Why add an embedded computer to a larger system?（为什么要用嵌入式？

  1. Better performance(更好的性能)

  2. More functions and features(更多功能特性)

  3. Lower cost (更低的消费)

  4. More dependability(更高的可靠性)

微处理器（MPU）和微控制器（MCU）

MPU: Typically defined as a single processor core (CPU) supports at least instruction fetching, decoding, and executing.(通常定义为单个cpu支持至少指令获取，解码和执行。)

A single MPU may have many CPUs.(单个mpu可能有多个cpu)

Normally for general-purpose computing, but needs to be supported with memories and I/Os（通常用于计算，而且需要io的支持和内存的支持。）

mcu：Typically has a MPU (typically a single core) with embedded peripherals e.g. memory blocks, Digital I/Os, Analogue I/Os, and other basic peripherals（通常具有mpu，并且嵌入了外围设备。

Embedded systems rely on both MCU hardware peripherals and software to get everything done on time

（嵌入式系统依赖于MCU硬件外设和软件内容，才能够按时完成所有任务。）

In most embedded systems, MCUs are chosen to be the best solution, since they offer（MCUs的优点）

• Low development and manufacturing cost（低的开发成本和制造成本。
• Easy porting and updating（容易移植和更新
• Light footprint（轻量级的构架
• Relatively low power consumption（相对低的功耗
• Satisfactory performance for low-end products（低端产品充足的性能

ARM processor families

Cortex-A series (Applications processors)：Support OS and high-performance applications e.g. smartphones, Smart TV, smart books

A系列（应用处理器）：支持操作系统和高性能应用，例如智能手机，电视或者书籍。

Cortex-R series (Real-time processors with high performance and high reliability)：Support real-time processing and mission-critical control e.g. automotive braking system, powertrains.

R系列（高性能可靠的实时处理器）：支持实时处理和任务关键控制，例如汽车制动，动力系统。

Cortex-M series (Microcontroller)：Cost-sensitive solutions for deterministic microcontroller applications e.g. smart sensors

M系列（微控制器）：针对确定性微控制器应用的低成本解决方案，例如智能传感器。

ARM Processors vs. ARM Architectures（ARM处理器和ARM架构）

ARM architecture(ARM架构)
• Describes the details of instruction set, programmer’s model, exception model, and memory map(描述指令集，程序员模型，异常模型和内容映射的详细信息)
• Documented in the Architecture Reference Manual（记录在架构参考手册之中）

ARM processor（ARM处理器）
• Developed using one of the ARM architectures（使用ARM结构之一开发）
• More implementation details, such as timing information（更多实现细节）
• Documented in processor’s Technical Reference Manual（记录在处理器的技术参考手册中）

Cortex-M4 Processor Memory Map（Cortex-M4 处理器内存映射）

Processor Registers（处理器寄存器）

R0 – R12: general purpose registers（通用寄存器）

Low registers (R0 – R7) can be accessed by any instruction（可以被任何指令访问）

High registers (R8 – R12) can only be accessed by some instructions（只能被某些指令访问）

R13: Stack Pointer (SP)（栈指针）

Records the current address of the stack（记录栈当前的地址）
Used for saving the context of a program while switching between tasks（用于在任务切换时保存程序上下文）

R14: Link Register (LR)（链接寄存器）

The LR is used to store the return address of a subroutine or a function call（LR用于·存储·1子程序或者·函数调用的返回地址）
The program counter (PC) will load the value from LR after a function is finished（函数执行完毕后，程序计数器会从LR加载值。）

xPSR: combined Program Status Register（组合程序状态寄存器）

Provides information about program execution and ALU flags（提供程序执行和ALU标志的信息）

Cortex-M4 Instruction Set（Cortex-M4 指令集）

Instruction Format（指令格式）

Labels（标签）：

Place marker, marking the memory address of the current instruction（放置标记，标记当前的指令内存地址）
Used by branch instructions to implement if-then or goto（被分支指令用于实现if-then or goto）
Must be unique（必须唯一）

Mnemonic（助记符）：

The name of the instruction（指令的名称）
Operation to be performed by processor core（处理器核心执行的操作）

Operands（操作数）：
Operands

Registers（寄存器）
Constants (called immediate values)（常量（称为立即值））

Number of operands varies（操作数的数量不同）

No operands（无操作数）: DSB
One operand（一个操作数）: BX LR
Two operands（两个操作数）: CMP R1, R2
Three operands（三个操作数）: ADD R1, R2, R3
Four operands（四个操作数）: MLA R1, R2, R3, R4

Normally（通常情况）

operand1 is the destination register, and operand2 and operand3 are source operands.

（operand1 是目标寄存器，operand2 和 operand3 是源操作数。）

operand2 is usually a register, and the first source operand

（operand2通常是寄存器，第一个源操作数。）

operand3 may be a register, an immediate number, a register shifted to a constant number of bits, or a register plus an offset (used for memory access)

（operand3可能是寄存器，立即数，寄存器左移常数位数或寄存器加偏移量（用于内存访问））

Comments（注释）

Everything after the semicolon (;) is a comment（分号（；）之后的内容都是注释。）

Explain programmers’ intentions or assumptions（用于解释意图或者假设）

ARM Assembly Language

Memory Access（内存访问）

STM32F401 which is based on the ARM Cortex-M4 core, supports only little-endian
memory format.

(STM32F401 基于 ARM Cortex-M4 内核，仅支持小端模式内存格式。)

In little-endian format, the least significant byte (LSB) is stored at the lowest memory
address, and the most significant byte (MSB) is stored at the highest memory address.

(在小端模式内存格式中，最低有效字节（LSB）存储在最低的内存地址，而最高有效字节（MSB）存储在最高的内存地址。)

Pre-index（前索引）

前索引是指在执行内存访问（加载或存储）之前，先计算目标地址，并将计算后的新地址更新到基址寄存器中，然后使用这个新地址进行内存访问。

Post-index（后索引）

后索引是指先使用基址寄存器中的当前值作为目标地址执行内存访问，然后在访问完成后，将基址寄存器更新为 Rn + offset。

Arithmetic: Addition（算数：加法）

64-bit addition with ADDS and ADC（64 位加法使用 ADDS 和 ADC）

Arithmetic: Subtraction（算数：减法）

96-bit subtraction with SUBS, SBCS and SBC（96 位减法使用 SUBS、SBCS 和 SBC）

Marketing

Six Types of New Products(六种新的产品)

1.新东西(New to the world)

2.新产品系列(New product lines)

3.现有产品系列的补充(Additions to existing product lines)

4.现有产品系列的改进和修正(Improvements and revisions to existing products)

5.重新定位(Re-positionings)

6.成本降低(Cost reductions)

What is New Product Development (NPD)?（什么是产品开发）

1.是创新的一个子过程，是创新过程的产出(a subprocess of innovation, the output of the innovation process)

2.是将商业机会转变为有形产品的过程(the process of transforming business opportunities into tangible profucts)

Reasons for a product failure（产品开发失败的原因）

1.产品没有新内容，或者没有进行优化(The product offers nothing new or no improved performance.)

2.缺少预算或者开发想法或者市场(lack the budget or ideas or market)

3.误解用户需求(misunderstanding of customers needs)

4.缺少管理支持(lack of management support)

5.缺少渠道(lack the channel)

6.入场太晚(late to market)

7.消费者口味或者时尚的突然变化(the changes in consumer tastes or fashion)

Product Development Process（产品开发流程）

1.Planning: make sure the target market for the product,business goals, key assumptions and limitation.

规划：明确产品的目标市场，商业目标，关键假设和限制。

2.Concept development : Identify the needs of the target market. Alternative product concepts are generated and evaluated. One or more concepts are selected for further development and testing.

概念开发：识别目标市场的需求。生成并评估替代产品的概念。选择一个或者多个概念进行进一步开发测试。

3.System-level design： A geometric layout of the product. A functional specification of each of the products subsystems. A preliminary process flow diagram for the final assembly process.

系统设计：产品的几何布局。每个产品子系统的功能规范。最终组装过程的流程图。

4.Detail design：the drawings or computer files describing the specifications of each of the
parts of the product and how it is to be assembled.

详细设计： 能够说明产品所有独特部件的完整规格和装配方法的文件内容。

5.Testing and refinement：Involves the construction and evaluation of multiple preproduction versions of the product. Further customer testing will be carried out at this stage.

测试与完善：涉及构建和评估产品的多个预生产版本。需要进一步的进行客户测试，并通过反馈改进产品。

6.Production ramp-up：Purpose of the ramp-up is to train the work force and to work out any remaining problems in the production process.

产能爬坡：通过培养人员来解决在生产过程之中遇到的所有的问题。

在整个产品开发的过程之中，任何一个阶段结束都应该做出决定是否继续开发。

Digitisation(数字化) vs. Digitalisation(数码化) vs. Digital Transformation(数字化转型)

Digitisation：the process of converting information from analog to digital.

将信息从物理格式转变为数字形式的过程，被称之为数字化。

Digitalisation：the process of using digitised information to make established ways of working simpler and more efficient.

将工作流程或者生产流程引入数字生产方式，从使得工作方式变得更加简单，高效的过程，被称之为数码化。

Digital Transformation：the process of using digital technologies to create new — or modify existing — business processes, culture, and customer experiences to meet changing business and market requirements.

通过数字技术创新新的，或者修改现有的业务流程或者市场，文化，用户画像，乃至于企业文化，从而不断地满足变化的业务和市场需求的过程，被称之为数字化转型。

Types of Digital Transformation（数字化转型的类型）

1.Process Transformation：aims to process such as data, analytics, AI, and any process that can work towards lowering costs and driving operational efficiency in the business.

流程转型：主要在处理数据，分析，人工智能等领域，以及任何能够降低成本并提高企业运营效率的过程。

2.Business Model Transformation：aims to make fundamental changes in how a business or organization runs which can include personnel, processes, and technology.

商业模式转型：主要是使企业或者组织的运行方式发生根本性变化，包括但不限于人员，流程或者技术。

3.Domain Transformation：This area offers a great opportunity to move into a new domain or area that a business may not have explored before by acquiring new technologies.

领域转型：企业通过获取新的技术，来进入一个可能尚未探索过的新的领域。

4.Cultural/Organizational Transformation：This is about redefining mindsets, processes, capabilities and skills for a digital world. It’s about driving digital transformation forward through growth initiatives that are grounded in a new culture and way of thinking.

文化,组织转型：主要是关于重新定义思维模式，流程，能力和技能，从而适应新的数字世界。需要通过以新文化或者新思维方式为基础的增长计划，来推动数字化转型。

Guidelines for a successful Digital Transformation（数字化转型的指南）

1.Understand your technology(理解你的技术)

2.Embrace Cultural Change(拥抱文化变化)

3.Consider a new digital business model(考虑新的数字商业模式)

4.Ensure Collaboration(考虑合作)

5.Top Management Support(高层管理支持)

Digital Product（数字产品）

1.No physical form, exist only in digital realm(没有物理形式存在，只存在于数字领域)

2.Intangible items delivered electronically.(能够以电子方式交付的无形物品)

3.anything that can be downloaded and used digitally can be considered a digital product(任何可以下载并用于数字的产品都可以被视为数字产品)

4.sold online or through brick-and-mortar retailers(可以在网络或者会实体销售的)

5.can be easily updated or modified to keep up with changing technology and trends because they’re intangible(可以轻松地更新或者修改以跟上不断变化的技术和趋势)

Will everything become digital?（什么东西都可以数字化吗？）

In practice, most products and experiences are hybrid.

A digital reproduction of a physical product is usually not the best way to go.

在实际实践之中，大多数产品和体验都是混合的。同时，对于一个实体产品进行数字复制往往并不是最佳选择。

Why digital products?（数字产品的优点）

1.Low investment, (potentially) high returns（低投入，同时还有着潜在的高回报）

2.Automated delivery for passive income（自动化交付从而实现被动的收入）

3.Digital products offer unique ways to communicate directly with the customers.（提供了能够直接和客户沟通的独特方式。）

Digital Project Development（数字产品开发）

Phase 1. Discovery：Discovery is the process of identifying the problem to be solved, making sure the problem is worth solving, and envisioning the solution to that problem.

发现：发现问题，并确保问题值得解决，并在一定程度上构想了该问题的解决方案的过程。

Phase 2. Ideate：The goal is to brainstorm possible solutions to the problem identified in the discovery phase, creating a strategy for how to build a product that will solve that problem.

想象：目标是针对发现阶段确定的问题，头脑风暴可能的解决方案，制定一个构建能够解决该问题的产品的策略。

Phase 3. Test：The testing phase is all about gathering data, refining and improving
your idea, and gathering more data until you have a sharp idea.

测试：测试阶段主要是收集数据、完善和改进你的想法，并不断收集更多数据，直到你有一个清晰的想法。

Phase 4. Execute：This stage aims to develop the “most valuable player” namely the Minimum Viable Product (MVP). And MVP is the product with the bare minimum of features and functionality needed to solve the problem identified in the discovery phase and validated in the testing phase.

执行：这一阶段旨在开发“最有价值的玩家”即最小可行产品（MVP）。也就是说找到一个具备解决发现阶段识别并在测试阶段验证的问题所需的最基本功能和特性的产品。

Phase 5. Launch：Once your MVP is ready to go, it’s time to launch.A digital product
launch usually means putting the MVP on the market and giving customers their first crack at your solution.Several approaches to this launch:

发布： 一旦你的最小可行产品（MVP）准备就绪，就该推出市场了。数字产品的推出通常意味着将 MVP 推向市场，让客户首次尝试你的解决方案。以下是几种推出方法：

Soft Launch—The MVP is made available to a limited segment of the target market, known as “early adopters.”

软发布—MVP 向目标市场的有限部分推出，被称为“早期采用者。”

Hard Launch—The MVP is made available to the market at large with a full marketing push (not recommended for MVP launches).

硬发布—MVP 全面推向市场，并配合全力营销推广（不建议用于 MVP 的发布）

Dark Launch—The MVP has already been launched, but new features get a “dark” launch to a select percentage of users to test their behavior in reaction to the new feature.

暗发布—MVP 已经发布，但新功能会先向特定百分比的用戶进行“暗”发布，以测试他们对新功能的反应行为。

Phase 6. Grow：As refine the digital product into its final form, based on the feedback from
the MVP launch, it’s time to consider the growth or scaling phase.

成长：将数字产品完善为其最终形态时，基于 MVP（最小可行产品）发布后的反馈，现在是考虑增长或扩展阶段的时候了。

Zeroing in on the Optimal Price. The MVP data could suggest that got the price a little too high, or even too low, especially with added features.

专注于最佳价格。MVP 数据可以建议价格稍微有点高，甚至有点低，尤其是加上附加功能后。

Scaling Up Marketing. the MVP leads can serve as valuable customer personas to create lookalike audiences to market to.

扩大营销规模。MVP 的潜在客户可以成为有价值的客户画像，用来创建相似受众进行营销。

Testing and More Testing. Phase 3 really never stops. At every stage of growth, keep validating the old hypotheses and new ones to make the product as viable as possible.
测试和更多测试。第三阶段实际上永远不会停止。在成长的每个阶段，都要验证旧的假设和新的假设，使产品尽可能可行。
